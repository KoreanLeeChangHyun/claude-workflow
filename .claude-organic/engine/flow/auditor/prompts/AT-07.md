# AT-07: Plan Completeness

## Evaluation Dimension

Assess whether the `plan.md` task list adequately covers the goals and targets
stated in the ticket — i.e., every major deliverable in the ticket has at least
one corresponding task in the plan.

## Scoring Criteria

| Score | Verdict | Criterion |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| 5     | PASS    | Every target deliverable in `ticket_xml` has a corresponding task in `plan_md`; no obvious gaps |
| 4     | PASS    | ≥ 80% of deliverables covered; minor gaps that do not affect core outcomes |
| 3     | WARN    | 50–79% coverage; one or more significant deliverables missing from plan |
| 2     | FAIL    | < 50% coverage; plan tasks do not reflect the stated targets |
| 1     | FAIL    | Plan is absent, has zero tasks, or tasks are unrelated to the ticket |

## Bias Mitigation

Compare against `<target>` in ticket_xml as the reference; do not penalize for
additional tasks in the plan beyond what is in target.
