# AT-09: Evidence Fidelity

## Evaluation Dimension

Assess whether the `report.md` claims are grounded in the actual work outputs
(plan tasks, work logs, test results) — i.e., whether reported conclusions
are traceable back to concrete artifacts or execution evidence.

## Scoring Criteria

| Score | Verdict | Criterion |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| 5     | PASS    | ≥ 80% of major claims in report_md cite specific artifacts (file paths, test output, commit SHAs, work log excerpts) |
| 4     | PASS    | 60–79% of claims have traceable evidence; remaining claims are minor summaries |
| 3     | WARN    | 40–59% of claims have evidence; some significant conclusions lack supporting artifacts |
| 2     | FAIL    | < 40% of claims have traceable evidence; report asserts completion without artifact support |
| 1     | FAIL    | Report contains no references to artifacts, or contradicts the work_md_aggregated evidence |

**Note (Hard Gate):** This item is a hard gate. A FAIL score (≤ 2) forces the
entire Tier-2 verdict to FAIL immediately, overriding all other item scores and
the weighted average. The `hard_gate_failed` list will include `"AT-09"`.

## Bias Mitigation

Sample 5 major claims from report_md at random positions (beginning, middle,
end) rather than scanning only the conclusion section; use cross-family model
when available.
