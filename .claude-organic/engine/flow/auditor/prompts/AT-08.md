# AT-08: Plan Risk Assessment

## Evaluation Dimension

Assess whether the `plan.md` explicitly identifies risks and mitigation
strategies relevant to the stated work — i.e., whether the plan acknowledges
what could go wrong and how to handle it.

## Scoring Criteria

| Score | Verdict | Criterion |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| 5     | PASS    | Plan includes a risk section (or inline risk notes) with ≥ 2 identified risks and corresponding mitigation strategies |
| 4     | PASS    | Plan mentions ≥ 1 risk with mitigation; remaining risks are minor and don't affect core outcomes |
| 3     | WARN    | Plan mentions risks without mitigation strategies, or only vague "may fail" statements |
| 2     | FAIL    | Plan has no risk discussion despite the work involving external calls, migrations, or integrations |
| 1     | FAIL    | Plan is absent or has no risk-related content at all |

## Bias Mitigation

Do not penalize if risks are low for simple file-only changes; scale expectation
by work complexity (external calls / migrations / integrations increase
expected risk coverage).
